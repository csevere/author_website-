PROPHET FROM THE MOON

A Journey into the Future

18-year-old Haitian-American Alyssira Beaulieu had a full ride to college before the bombs fell and destroyed her life.

Three hundred years after the destruction, she awakes in the streets of an unfamiliar Boston in a whole new body. Lost and buffeted by shock, she searches for help but gets kidnapped by the city’s most notorious gang. A boy who goes by the name of Prophet from the Moon rescues Alyssira and leads her to his home, a compound of soldiers led by an ambitious young captain and her handsome lieutenant. Alyssira has only one goal: to survive. She imagines a second chance at life in the refuge, falling for the captain, training as a soldier, and becoming a close sibling to Prophet.

But a cosmic entity threatens her dreams when it reveals she is part of intergalactic order of guardians called Curators, souls of the dead infused into supernatural bodies. It instructs her to cross a perilous wasteland to Toronto to find a journal capable of stopping elites living in cities on the Moon from selling Earth to an alien race. But the captain plans to attack the compound's enemies and needs Alyssira’s help too. Now loyal to her new family, Alyssira must decide whether to fight for her compound’s future or abandon her dreams and loved ones to fulfill her purpose as Curator of Earth.

"Why should I gamble my life and Prophet’s for false hope?"

A cynical young woman highly versed in sarcasm, Alyssira "Aly" Beaulieu is a cool intellectual. She's fiercely loyal to her loved ones and has a passionate sense of justice, making her take risks she usually wouldn’t take. This is also her weakness because she gets herself into situations that prove too overwhelming. She has a chill nature but can take things too personally.
